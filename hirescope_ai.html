Contact Information

This is the header of your resume — make it complete and professional.

💡 Pro tip: Include LinkedIn and GitHub — recruiters at top companies always check these. A complete profile gets 40% more callbacks.

Professional Summary

3–4 lines that sell you instantly. This is what recruiters read first — make it count.

💡 Formula: [Role] with [X years] in [domain]. [Top achievement with numbers]. [Key skill or passion].
✦ AI Generated — Personalized for you

Education

List your highest degree first. Include GPA/percentage if above 7.5 or 75%.

💡 Always include institution, degree, year, and GPA (if strong). IIT/NIT/BITS/IIIT alumni: institution name alone is a signal — keep it prominent.

Professional Experience

Use the format: Job Title | Company | Date Range, then bullet points. Quantify everything.

💡 STAR format wins: Start with an action verb, include what you did, and always end with impact (numbers, %, time saved, users affected).
✦ AI Enhanced — Action verbs + quantified

Skills & Certifications

Group by category for maximum ATS impact. Include tools, frameworks, languages, and platforms.

💡 Categories matter: Languages / Frameworks / Cloud & DevOps / Databases / Tools. ATS systems score higher when skills are grouped logically.
✦ AI Organized — ATS-optimized grouping
💡 Include year. Cloud certifications (AWS, GCP, Azure) are highly valued by Indian MNCs and product companies.

Projects, Awards & Languages

Projects are often the deciding factor for freshers. Show real work with real numbers.

💡 Format: Project Name | Tech Stack then bullets. Include GitHub link or live URL if available. Numbers = credibility.
✦ AI Enhanced — Impact-focused bullets
💡 Language skills matter for global roles. Always mention English proficiency level.
